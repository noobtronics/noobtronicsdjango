About Us

noobtronics™ is a project started by IIT Bombay Electrical Engineering alumni


At noobtronics, we manufacture Open Source hardware and also make hardware of our own, for noobs and pros alike.


All the products that are branded as 'noobtronics' are designed to the state-of-the-art and manufactured by us from scratch. Each of the manufactured product goes through Extensive Integration Testing designed specifically for that product to ensure our customers always get the specs claimed on our website.
