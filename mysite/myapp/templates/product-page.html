Hi

Smartduino Uno is an entry level microcontroller which you can use in your electronics project. It is based on Atmel's Atmega 328 chip. It has 14 digital input/output pins (of which 6 can be used as PWM outputs), 6 analog inputs, a 20 MHz quartz crystal, a USB connection, a power jack, an ICSP header and a reset button.

Features:

  • Cost effective and 25% faster than Arduino UNO
  • microUSB Connector - now you won't have to worry about losing the scarce USB cable
Microcontroller Atmel's Atmega 328
Computing Speed 20 MIPS
Clock Rate 20 Mhz
Flash Memory 32 KBytes
SRAM 2 KBytes
EEPROM 1 KBytes
Digital I/O Pins 14
Analog Input Pins 6
PWM Output Pins (Analog) 6
Operating Voltage 5V
Power Source microUSB (5V)
or
Power Jack (6-16V)

  • 1 Smartduino Uno Board
  • 1 microUSB Cable
  • 1 LED for testing
